# Requirements Interview Guide

## Always capture
- stakeholder identity and role
- business goals
- actors
- trigger
- preconditions
- success outcome
- main success scenario
- alternate flows
- exception flows
- non-functional requirements
- assumptions
- open questions

## Good interviewer prompts
- What starts this process?
- Who initiates it?
- What must already be true before it can begin?
- What does success look like?
- What normally happens first, next, and last?
- What variations happen in practice?
- What can go wrong?
- What performance, security, compliance, or usability expectations apply?
